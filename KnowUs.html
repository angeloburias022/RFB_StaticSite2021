History

RFB Airconditioning Services “the company” is a sole proprietor and established by Rafael B. Burias on April 13, 2016 as air-conditioning services provider. In 10 years, the owner was employed as aircon and electrical technician in a reputable and stable company. In 2016, the owner saw the large market of outsourcing so he established business and decided to seek more aircon technician/helper and cater large establishments/businesses. The company encourages the safety of workers and safe of place practice. The company also focuses on the customer-satisfaction and continuous improvement of services.
